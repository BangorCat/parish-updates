# Project of the Community Nonprofit Website

This project is created to share information for the community. Content is published under responsible use—no copyrighted or harmful material—and personal data is not collected without consent.

## Purpose

Provides the information of events, readings, and remembrances for our local community, and urgent messages that notify when printed newsletters are out of date or when cancellations or other unexpected situations occur and the community needs to be informed.

## Who We Are

Volunteer-run local community information resource focused on sharing schedules, announcements, and remembrances for neighbors and parishioners.

## Content Policy

- Content must be responsibly sourced. No copyrighted material unless permission is granted.
- No harmful or illegal material.
- Respectful and constructive tone required for all submissions.

## Privacy

Personal data is not collected without consent.

## Notice About Printed Newsletters

Although a printed newsletter is provided in person, schedules sometimes change; when updates or cancellations are not reflected in the printed newsletter, the site provides timely notices so visitors can learn when services are cancelled, rescheduled, or otherwise changed.

## Developer Note

This project was created as a training exercise to refresh the developer’s skills during the Easter break. Volunteers maintain the site and handle content submissions and updates.
